Editor's Pick: The Future of Cities

Urban planners across the globe are rethinking how cities should be designed for future generations. With populations growing rapidly, space is becoming more precious than ever.

Smart city technologies are being implemented in dozens of major cities, using sensors and AI to manage traffic, reduce energy consumption, and improve public safety.

From vertical gardens to underground transport systems, the city of tomorrow looks very different from what we know today. Architects and engineers are collaborating like never before.

Community spaces are being reimagined as multi-purpose areas that serve different functions throughout the day — a market in the morning, a park in the afternoon, an event venue at night.

Sustainability is at the heart of every new urban development project, with green rooftops, solar panels, and rainwater harvesting systems becoming standard features.
